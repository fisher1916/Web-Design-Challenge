Wind Speed

Most cities are in places with a wind speed of 10mph or less. This is expected, as higher wind speeds would make farming and maintaining housing more difficult. As seen in other graphs, most cities in this dataset are in the northern hemisphere. However, for the reasons this result was expected, it is unlikely the conclusions would be different given more cities in the southern hemisphere.
